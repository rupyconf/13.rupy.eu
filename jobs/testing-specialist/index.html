Testing Specialist

Testing Specialist - 1201809 Job Location: Hungary-Hungary-Budapest

Description

HP Application Services Team is seeking to identify highly motivated and qualified individuals for various future opportunities as Testing Specialists.

In anticipation of these opportunities becoming available, completing a profile will allow us to evaluate your qualifications and interests and will allow us to stay connected.

About HP

HP is the world’s largest provider of information, technology infrastructure, software, services and solution to individuals and organizations of all sizes. HP is committed to the development of each of its employees, equipping you with all the skills for an international career. We concentrate on both ‘soft' and technical skills, placing heavy emphasis on ‘on the job’ learning as well as formal instruction.

About the Organization

Our Applications Services unit helps client organizations to plan, develop, integrate and manage custom made applications and industry-specific solutions in the Communications area. Our team includes groups of developers, testers and a project team.

Main responsibilities

  • Participate in the testing of new releases of the Sales-support applications
  • Perform test planning, test review and test administration activities
  • Work with Scrum methodology, use agile methods

Qualifications

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher completed within the field of Informatics, Programming or Electrical Engineering prior to start date
  • 3-5 years of experience in Telco sector
  • English knowledge (good written and communication)
  • Good analytical skills
  • Ability to work in test teams (cooperation in technical and personal aspects)
  • Ability to write test related documentations (Design, Test Run Report, Test Summary Report etc.)
  • Systematic thinking, good problem resolution
  • Ability to work in full time
  • Highly motivated, proactive personality

Advantage

  • Experience with Amdocs Billing System
  • Experience with eCommerce systems
  • Experience in Software testing
  • Experience in test automation in Fitnesse or other tools

More Details
